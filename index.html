VAE Image Linker

VAEを使って二つの画像をつなぐよ。
二つの画像を潜在空間で線形に繋ぎ、その間の画像を生成するよ。
あまり良いモデルではないので、再構成はガバガバだよ。

Demonstration of linking two uploaded images using VAE: Variational Autoencoder.
The images are linearly linked in the latent space, and the images between them are generated.
The reconstruction is not so good because it is not a good model.

Details of the model architecture, training, etc: misya11p/vae-image-linker
